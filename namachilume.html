Namada chilume

Namada Chilume is a popular tourist destination located in the midst of a forested area in the Tumkur district of Karnataka, India. It is a natural spring that originates from the rocky surface of a hill and is known for its crystal clear water

The name "Namada Chilume" means "spring of medicinal water" in the Kannada language, and the water of the spring is believed to have medicinal properties. It is said that the water of the spring is rich in minerals and has therapeutic value for various ailments. The water is also considered sacred, and many devotees come to take a dip in the spring during auspicious occasions. Apart from its medicinal properties, Namada Chilume is also known for its natural beauty. The spring is surrounded by a lush green forest, and the area around the spring is a popular spot for picnics and day trips. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll through the forest and observe the flora and fauna of the region, including several species of birds and animals. Namada Chilume is also a popular destination for trekking and camping. The hill from where the spring originates offers a great view of the surrounding landscape, and visitors can enjoy the breathtaking view of the sunset from the hilltop. Visitors can also set up their tents and camp in the forested area around the spring. The place is also associated with a popular legend. It is believed that Lord Rama, the protagonist of the Hindu epic, Ramayana, visited this place during his exile in the forest. It is said that Lord Rama shot an arrow into the rocky surface of the hill, and a spring of water emerged from the spot where the arrow hit the rock. The spot where Lord Rama shot the arrow is still preserved and is a popular spot for tourists to visit. Overall, Namada Chilume is a great place to visit for those seeking a break from the city's hustle and bustle. The place offers visitors a chance to connect with nature, explore the region's rich history and culture, and enjoy the medicinal properties of the spring water. With its tranquil atmosphere, natural beauty, and rich cultural heritage, Namada Chilume is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Tumkur.
